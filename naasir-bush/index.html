About Naasir

Naasir Bush is a results-driven IT leader with over a decade of experience in software development, team leadership, and process improvement. He currently serves as Manager of Frontend Development at Corporate Tools LLC, where he has consistently demonstrated leadership and technical excellence. He excels at bridging the gap between technology and business strategy, ensuring teams are aligned with organizational goals while driving innovation and operational excellence.

He holds an MBA in IT Management from Western Governors University and a B.S. in Software Development (Western Governors University, 2022). He is also on track to complete an M.S. in Management & Leadership (July 2025). This demonstrates his commitment to continuous learning and leadership development.

His core strengths include strategic planning, technical analysis, implementation coordination, project management, and talent development. He is also bilingual in English and Arabic, enabling him to connect with a diverse range of colleagues and clients.
